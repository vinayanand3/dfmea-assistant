# WS-JOIN-017 Synthetic BIW Weld Standard

Structural BIW weld pitch shall be set using load path and fatigue demand. Static strength checks alone are not enough for front rail, rocker, shock tower, and underbody attachment joints.

For high-strength steel front rail flanges, weld pitch in a CAE fatigue hot spot should be 35 mm or tighter unless an approved durability analysis shows equivalent life. Marker: AFR-WELD-PITCH-35MM.

Spot weld nugget diameter shall be verified by teardown or non-destructive audit during Alpha and Beta builds. For the front rail flange durability validation, the minimum nugget size is 5.5 mm.

Weld gun access shall be checked before tooling release. Any inaccessible weld must be moved, converted to an alternate joining process, or flagged for manufacturing review.
