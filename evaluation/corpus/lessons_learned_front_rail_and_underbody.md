# Synthetic BIW Lessons Learned

## Lesson LL-AFR-001: front rail crash bulkhead

A prior Alpha front rail program showed crash load-path folding outside the intended crush zone during barrier test. The section near the shotgun interface was locally too soft and lacked an internal bulkhead. Confirm the crush initiator location, add a local internal bulkhead when CAE shows off-target folding, and require CAE correlation plus physical crash teardown. Marker: FR-CRASH-BULKHEAD-ALPHA.

## Lesson LL-AFR-002: weld pitch from fatigue maps

Rail flange spot weld fatigue occurred when weld pitch was selected from static strength tables instead of a cyclic strain hot-spot map. Use 35 mm pitch or tighter in the high-strain region and confirm by durability teardown. Marker: AFR-WELD-PITCH-35MM.

## Lesson LL-UBB-001: underbody bracket edge distance

Underbody battery bracket pull-out occurred during shaker durability because fastener edge distance was only 1.6 times hole diameter. Use edge distance of at least 2.5 times hole diameter, add local embossing, and link the risk to a pull-out test plus shaker durability. Marker: AFR-EDGE-2P5D.
