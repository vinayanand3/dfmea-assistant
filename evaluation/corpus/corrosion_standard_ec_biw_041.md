# EC-BIW-041 Synthetic E-Coat and Corrosion Standard

Closed BIW sections exposed to road splash shall include at least two 12 mm drain or vent holes when geometry can trap fluid. The holes shall be positioned to drain after e-coat dip. Marker: AFR-DRAIN-12MM.

Internal overlap flanges shall show e-coat film build of at least 10 microns on section cuts. Any area below 10 microns requires geometry, venting, or process changes before validation closure.

Front rail closed overlap flanges require 96-cycle corrosion validation with teardown sectioning. No red rust is permitted before 60 cycles, and no perforation is permitted at any point.
